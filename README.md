### Project Conclusion: Heart Health Analysis

## What I Did

Data Exploration & Profiling: I performed an initial inspection of the heart disease dataset, identifying key features such as age, sex, chest pain types, and blood pressure. I also checked for missing values to ensure data integrity.

Demographic Analysis: I calculated the average age of patients and analyzed the gender distribution within the study group to identify the primary demographics affected.

Clinical Metric Evaluation: I analyzed critical health markers, including average resting blood pressure and the prevalence of high fasting blood sugar (levels > 120 mg/dl), to assess the risk profiles of the patients.

Categorical Segmentation: I identified and quantified different types of chest pain recorded in the dataset, providing insights into common symptoms associated with heart health issues.

Advanced Analytical Querying: I structured the analysis into basic, intermediate, and advanced levels to systematically answer complex questions regarding patient health trends and correlations.

## Tools I Used


Python: The core language used for the analysis.

Pandas & NumPy: I used these libraries for data manipulation, cleaning, and calculating statistical summaries like averages and value counts.

Matplotlib & Seaborn: I utilized these for data visualization to better understand the distribution and relationships between health metrics.
